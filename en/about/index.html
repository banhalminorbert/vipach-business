About

We do not simply build visibility. We build digital entities that can be understood, referenced and trusted.

VIPACH for Business is the business direction of Norbert Banhalmi, based in Vienna and created for Hungarian companies, founders, experts and brands entering the DACH and US markets. The goal is not merely to create a beautiful website or a strong portrait. The goal is to make a company or personal brand understandable, searchable, credible and recommendable — for people, search engines and AI systems alike.

Founder background

Norbert Banhalmi is first and foremost a photographer and visual positioning specialist. His LinkedIn positioning is: Executive Portraiture • Visual Branding Strategist • Helping leaders build a powerful professional presence. His work has been shaped by Budapest, Vienna, Munich and New York. The New York experience is not only a photographic influence; it is also a business mindset: to understand quickly what makes a leader, personal brand or story internationally readable and valuable.

VIPACH for Business

VIPACH for Business is not the same as the VIPACH creative photography community, and it is not a service of the Central Association of Hungarian Associations and Organisations in Austria. It is the business direction founded by Norbert Banhalmi: Norbert’s primary field is executive portraiture, visual branding and visual positioning, while entity building is handled by the VIPACH for Business team through strategic photography, web development, content architecture, structured data and AI-compatible brand building.

Who we help

We primarily support Hungarian companies, personal brands and founders who want to move from the Hungarian market into Austria, Germany, Switzerland or a broader international environment. The emphasis is not on loud promises, but on a verifiable digital structure: clear positioning, premium visual presence, multilingual content, search visibility and AI readability.

AmCham Austria

Bánhalmi Norbert e.U. is an official member of AmCham Austria and is connected to the official WKO / Vienna Chamber of Commerce environment. This should not be presented as a certification, endorsement or partner status, but as business-community and chamber presence that gives us a more direct view of Austrian, DACH and transatlantic business thinking. It helps us keep a finger on the pulse of global market developments.
